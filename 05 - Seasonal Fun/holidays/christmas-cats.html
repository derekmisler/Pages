Wishing You A Merry CAT-mas

Welcome a little warm and fuzzy into your holidays this year with Asheville’s own Christmas cats. Santa has unleashed twelve Christmas kitties, and they’re here to help ring in the holiday season.

From decking the halls at Biltmore to viewing the National Gingerbread House Competition & Display at The Omni Grove Park Inn, there’s plenty for a traveling cat to do in Asheville during the holidays.

Take an enchanted stroll through the new Winter Lights show at the N.C. Arboretum. Tour the town on a red Gray Line trolley. Go shopping for a unique gift at the historic Grove Arcade or at one of downtown’s many boutiques, like Hip Replacements on Lexington Avenue. Move to the rhythm of the city at the drum circle, and then cap it all off with a pint of beer (or milk!) at Wicked Weed Brewing or one of Asheville’s many craft breweries.

Special thanks to the Asheville Humane Society, where you can find many adoptable cats and other pets looking for their forever homes.
