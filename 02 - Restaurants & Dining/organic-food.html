Asheville is paradise for lovers of organic and vegetarian food, with local, organic cuisine a staple at many restaurants and several tailgate markets. Asheville has been honored by GoVeg.com and Organic Style magazine. This is a place to find wonderful, satisfying and gourmet cuisine that is hormone-free, pesticide-free, antibiotic-free, meat-free and fabulous!

Organic and Vegetarian Restaurants

There are numerous restaurants in Asheville that feature organic cuisine, vegetarian dishes or vegan options. At the popular Tupelo Honey Café, diners encounter delectable Southern home-style cooking with all organic ingredients. The menu also features great organic choices for kids. At West End Bakery, the pastries and breads are all made from scratch with 100 percent organic flour. South Street Cakes offers all-natural, made-from-scratch, southern-style cakes delivered to your door free of charge inside Asheville city limits.

Rosetta's Kitchen offers up "Whole Food Soul Food" with organic, vegetarian and vegan comfort foods. The Laughing Seed Café is an all-vegetarian restaurant featuring fine cuisine crafted with locally-grown ingredients. Also, be sure to check out Plant, Asheville's first full-service, completely vegan restaurant.

Organic Produce

Asheville is well-known for its fresh, locally-grown produce. There are several tailgate markets, many located at organic grocery stores. Asheville tailgate markets are special places, perfect for visitors who want an authentic taste of Western North Carolina. Our tailgate markets are about more than just food. They're about reconnecting with the land and meeting the people who devote their lives to bringing food from the farm to your tabletop. See a list of Asheville tailgate and organic markets.
