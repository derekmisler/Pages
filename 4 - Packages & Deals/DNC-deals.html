Nestled in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains, amid artist studios and craft breweries, farm-to-table restaurants and traditional American tunes, Asheville awaits.

It's the place George Vanderbilt III chose to build America's Largest Home­-the Biltmore. It's home to some of the most stops along America's Favorite Drive-the Blue Ridge Parkway. And it was the romantic weekend getaway of choice for President Obama and the First Lady when they needed some time away from it all. Want to follow in their footsteps?
