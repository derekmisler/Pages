Handcrafted Holidays in Asheville

The holiday season is a special one in Asheville. When visiting let the festive spirit take you! Whether your tastes run to theater, shopping or simply fine dining, you will find it in abundance. Visit the Biltmore House’s renowned candlelight Christmas display harkening back to a time when life moved by at a slower—and gracious pace. Take in a live performance at the Diana Wortham Theatre, revel in wonder at holiday light displays at the North Carolina Arboretum, browse downtown galleries and stroll artists’ studios at the River Arts District. Shopping for family and friends—or yourself—has never been so enjoyable. Designer boutiques and galleries showcasing one-of-a-kind handmade arts and crafts abound at every turn. After a day of shopping and sightseeing you can dine on whatever cuisine your heart desires from one of the 250+ independent restaurants. Stay awhile and enjoy holiday magic in the mountains.
