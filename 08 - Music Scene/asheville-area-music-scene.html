Asheville Area Music Scene

Asheville’s music scene is born of age old traditions, nurtured by a spirit of independence and the muse of the mountain skyline. The embracing atmosphere reflects a sense of place, harmonic yet playfully divergent and diverse. In a scene that’s on the verge, Asheville’s talented wellspring of music lovers and creators offer the musically minded traveler multi-layered experiences, innovative sounds and the irresistible urge to move. Play on in, won’t you?

Venues

Asheville's thriving music scene means that literally hundreds of bands and musicians make their way through the city during the year. The best way to tap into Asheville's live music is to experience it first-hand at one of our local venues.

Festivals

From the local and national acts that performed at Moogfest and Mountain Oasis, to the global performers at July's Folkmoot festival in nearby Waynesville, the Asheville area's summer music festivals are as eclectic as the city itself.
