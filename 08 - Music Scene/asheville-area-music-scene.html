Asheville Area Music Scene

Asheville’s music scene is born of age old traditions, nurtured by a spirit of independence and the muse of the mountain skyline. The embracing atmosphere reflects a sense of place, harmonic yet playfully divergent and diverse. In a scene that’s on the verge, Asheville’s talented wellspring of music lovers and creators offer the musically minded traveler multi-layered experiences, innovative sounds and the irresistible urge to move. Play on in, won’t you?

Making Music in the Blue Ridge Mountains
The sounds of bluegrass and old-time music have their roots in the Appalachian mountain region around Asheville, N.C., where visitors can hear live music any night of the week: www.ExploreAsheville.com/music Asheville’s many festivals and diverse venues keep the area’s musical heritage alive and make it a unique destination for lovers of both traditional and contemporary music.
