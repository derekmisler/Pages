The Asheville Arts Alive website offers two main features:

  • An interactive calendar of
    arts-related events

  • A directory of arts amenities, artists, organizations and businesses

Each presenting participant will have the opportunity to create a FREE individual profile page enabling arts partners to attract visitors with images, video, events, special offers, tours, workshops, hours of operation, directions, and the opportunity to share what’s special about their unique offerings. Visitor-oriented listings may also appear on ExploreAsheville.com, the area’s official tourism website that attracts more than 3.8 million visitors annually.

Use this valuable tool to reach over 9 million visitors who are drawn to Buncombe County each year and are looking for what is unique and special, so that they can take home great memories and more!

Criteria

  1. Do you or your business reside in Buncombe County?
    Are your employees primarily Buncombe County residents?
  2. Is your product unique, handcrafted, and produced primarily in Buncombe County?
  3. Do you produce ticketed cultural events in Buncombe County?
  4. Is your business or product accessible during posted business hours?
  5. Do you offer classes or workshops in which the public can participate?
  6. Do you provide a regular opportunity for the public to purchase or view
    Buncombe County creative product?
  7. Do you or your business facilitate the enjoyment of Buncombe County
    arts, culture, and/or entertainment?
