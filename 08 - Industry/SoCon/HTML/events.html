  • SoCon Overtime at the Grove Arcade

    Date: March 6, 2015

    Time: 6:30–9:30 p.m.

    Location: Grove Arcade

    Address: 1 Page Avenue, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Join us at the beautiful, historic Grove Arcade on Page Ave, just a block away from the US Cellular Center for SoCon OverTime. A party open to all, SoCon OverTime is an event not to miss, it is a welcome party and pep rally all rolled into one. SoCon OverTime is a perfect opportunity to take a break from the games and see and taste a little bit of Asheville.

    The event will feature several of our famous local restaurants and breweries. Trade the tokens you purchase at the door for pints and small bites from some of our favorite eateries and drinking spots, then go back to the US Cellular Center for more basketball or out on the town for more food and fun, either way you win!

  • Downtown Dribble

    Date: March 7, 2015

    Time: 11–11:30 a.m.

    Registration: 9:30 a.m.

    Location: Vance Monument

    Address: Pack Square, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Call us crazy, but we are inviting 500 kids to Vance Monument and give them all free t-shirts and basketballs and then everyone is participating in a dribble parade to the US Cellular Center. Participants will also get in free to the men’s basketball session at the US Cellular Center that starts at Noon.

  • Arc of Buncombe County 5k

    Date: March 7, 2015

    Time: 9:30–10:30 a.m.

    Address: Pack Square, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Get in your morning workout during the Championships and help contribute to a great cause! The second annual Arc of Buncombe County 5K will be held in conjunction with the Downtown Dribble on Saturday morning, March 7. The start and finish of the race will take place at Pack Square. The Arc of Buncombe County is an organization that assists individuals with intellectual and development disabilities.
