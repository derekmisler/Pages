  • SoCon Overtime at the Grove Arcade

    Date: March 6, 2015

    Time: 6:30–9:30 p.m.

    Location: Grove Arcade

    Address: 1 Page Avenue, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Join us at the beautiful, historic Grove Arcade on Page Ave, just a block away from the US Cellular Center for SoCon OverTime. A party open to all, SoCon OverTime is an event not to miss, it is a welcome party and pep rally all rolled into one. SoCon OverTime is a perfect opportunity to take a break from the games and see and taste a little bit of Asheville.

    The event will feature several of our famous local restaurants and breweries. Trade the tokens you purchase at the door for pints and small bites from some of our favorite eateries and drinking spots, then go back to the US Cellular Center for more basketball or out on the town for more food and fun, either way you win!

  • Downtown Dribble

    Date: March 7, 2015

    Time: 11–11:30 a.m.

    Registration: 9:30 a.m.

    Location: Vance Monument

    Address: Pack Square, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Call us crazy, but we are inviting 500 kids to Vance Monument and give them all free t-shirts and basketballs and then everyone is participating in a dribble parade to the US Cellular Center. Participants will also get in free to the men’s basketball session at the US Cellular Center that starts at Noon.

  • Arc of Buncombe County 5k

    Date: March 7, 2015

    Time: 9:30–10:30 a.m.

    Address: Pack Square, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Get in your morning workout during the Championships and help contribute to a great cause! The second annual Arc of Buncombe County 5K will be held in conjunction with the Downtown Dribble on Saturday morning, March 7. The start and finish of the race will take place at Pack Square. The Arc of Buncombe County is an organization that assists individuals with intellectual and development disabilities.

SoCon Championship Special Theme Days

  • Opening Day

    Date: March 5, 2015

    Location: Kimmel Arena

    Address: 227 Campus Drive, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Join us for opening day of the 2015 SoCon Basketball Championships at Kimmel Arena and enjoy special opening day ticket prices. In partnership with Eblen Charities and Hoops Against Hunger, join the fight against childhood hunger by bringing a non-perishable, easy to prepare food item to donate to Hoops Against Hunger. Learn more about this program at www.hoopsagainsthunger.net.

  • Education Day & Educator Appreciation Night

    Date: March 6, 2015

    Location: US Cellular Center

    Address: 87 Haywood Street, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: FREE field trip admission for nearly 3,000 local elementary school students for the 11am game. Education Day is an Asheville SoCon tradition and fan favorite, as it provides a great opportunity to expose the young people in our community to student athletes and high energy collegiate championship play. Local private schools, homeschooling groups and area educators will receive special pricing incentives to attend the afternoon and evening games.

  • Heroes Day

    Date: March 7, 2015

    Location: US Cellular Center

    Address: 87 Haywood Street, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Special ticket prices are available to first responders, military staff and veterans. In partnership with local fire and law enforcement departments, the concourse will feature a community safety fair. Emergency personnel from several departments will be on hand with Identa-kid kits, McGruff the Crime Dog, Sparky the Fire Dog, information on smoke detectors and family emergency action plans and more. The day will also feature the US Air Force Color Guard and a performance or two by the Asheville Fire Fighter Pipe & Drum band.

  • Family Day

    Date: March 8, 2015

    Location: US Cellular Center

    Address: 87 Haywood Street, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Special family-pack pricing incentives on tickets and food. Special pricing and activities for local youth sports teams. In partnership with the YMCA, Y staff will offer fun fitness focused activities and games on the concourse throughout the day.

  • Pack the House

    Date: March 9, 2015

    Location: US Cellular Center

    Address: 87 Haywood Street, Asheville, NC 28801

    Details: Special "locals only" pricing. Food and beverage specials. A pre-game concert, and a little bit of Asheville on the concourse will make this championship game the place to be on Monday night. Help “pack the house” for the nationally televised Monday night men’s championship game on ESPN2.
