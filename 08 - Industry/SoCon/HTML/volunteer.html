Check-in Entrance at the men's tournament

March 8-11, 2013, U.S. Cellular Center

  • Welcome event volunteers, members of the media, school bands, cheer and dance teams. Once you have checked their credentials you will direct them to the areas where they will be working or where they can store their equipment and gather before their game begins.

  • The shifts are approx. 3½ hours in length and you will receive tournament apparel to wear if you sign up for two shifts.

Children's Activity Center Volunteer at the men's tournament

March 8-11, 2013, U.S. Cellular Center

  • Wanna play? Be close to the action and have fun in the children's activity zone. Join the staff and children by participating in age appropriate sports games like pop a shot basketball and health and wellness games.

  • The shifts are approx. 3½ hours in length and you will receive tournament apparel to wear if you sign up for two shifts. Background checks will be required.

Cheer/Band/Team/Officials Host at the women's tournament

March 8-11, 2013, Kimmel Arena on the campus of UNC Asheville

  • Welcome the teams, cheer squads, school band and officials as they arrive at the arena, check their credentials and show them to the appropriate areas before their game begins.

  • The shifts are approx. 3½ hours in length and you will receive tournament apparel to wear if you sign up for two shifts.

Media and Volunteer Gate at the women's tournament

March 8-11, 2013, Kimmel Arena on the campus of UNC Asheville

  • Welcome event volunteers and members of the media. Once you have checked their credentials you will direct them to the areas where they will be working. You will also be answering any visitor information questions and distributing maps and travel guides to out of town visitors as needed.

  • The shifts are approx. 3½ hours in length and you will receive tournament apparel to wear if you sign up for two shifts.

Downtown Dribble

March 9, 2013, Downtown Asheville

  • Come out and be a part of the Downtown Dribble on Saturday morning, March 9. Volunteers will be needed to help with distributing basketballs and t-shirts to participating children, as well as, being a part of the downtown dribble which will wind through the streets of downtown Asheville and end at the U.S. Cellular Center. The shifts is approx. 4 hours in length and you will receive tournament apparel to wear if you sign up for two shifts.

All volunteers will be notified in early February of the times available for volunteer orientation sessions at either Asheville's U.S. Cellular Center or Kimmel Arena. These sessions will provide you with important information about volunteer expectations and duties, parking and tournament details.
