The road to the Final Four starts in Asheville. Don’t miss this opportunity to see your favorite SoCon men’s and women’s basketball teams in action on the court. This year tournament play doesn’t just end with basketball — we are rolling out the wrestling mats to welcome SoCon wrestling teams as well, that’s right — wrestling! We are thrilled to add the Southern Conference Wrestling Championship to the already fun and full tournament week.

Enjoy spending time in a city you’ve heard so much about. Tournament week runs March 5–9, 2015 at The US Cellular Center and UNC Asheville’s Kimmel Arena. Both venues are located near excellent dining and shopping. Come for the game or match and stay for…Asheville, a city with something for everyone.
