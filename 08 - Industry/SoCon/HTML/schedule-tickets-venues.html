Venues

ExploreAsheville.com Arena

87 Haywood St.

Asheville, NC 28801

The recently renovated ExploreAsheville.com Arena in the US Cellular Center is a great venue for watching a game with a seating capacity for over 6,000 fans. The US Cellular Center is located in the heart of Asheville’s vibrant downtown shopping and dinning district.

View the ExploreAsheville.com Arena seating chart and order your tickets online here.

UNC Asheville Kimmel Arena

One University Heights

Asheville, NC 28804

Kimmel Area is a state-of-the-art facility with seating for 3,200 fans and is the perfect venue to get the SoCon Women’s Basketball tournament started. Join us there for the quarterfinals games on Thursday March 5, 2015.

Kimmel Arena will then be transformed as we sweep the basketball court and roll out wrestling mats to accommodate the Inaugural 2015 SoCon Wrestling Championship on Saturday March 7, 2015.
