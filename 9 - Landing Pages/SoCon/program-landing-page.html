7 Ideas for Discovering Asheville This Weekend

Step outside the arena and experience all the city of Asheville has to offer. Adventures await you, even within walking distance of where you are sitting right now.

  • Food

    Taste your way through our Foodtopian Society—check out some of our great independent restaurants.

  • Shopping

    Shop for vintage and locally designed clothing, housewares, art and souvenirs on Lexington Avenue, or in the historic Grove Arcade building.

  • Chocolate

    Warm up with a Liquid Truffle from the French Broad Chocolate Lounge, or satisfy your sweet tooth at one of our other great chocolatiers.

  • History of Downtown

    See the sights of Downtown Asheville as you learn about the city's history on the self-guided Asheville Urban Trail.

  • Arts and Crafts

    Explore Asheville's arts and crafts heritage in one of downtown's 30+ art galleries.

  • Tours

    See Asheville from a new perspective with one of our city tours. Go by trolley, by bus, on foot or by bike.

  • Blue Ridge Parkway

    Get out of town and into the Blue Ridge Mountains with a drive on the Blue Ridge Parkway.
