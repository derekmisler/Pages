Nestled in a verdant Blue Ridge Mountain valley and framed by the highest peaks along the Blue Ridge Parkway, Asheville, NC has become the craft-brewing center of the Southeast. Asheville beer has garnered so much enthusiasm that it was named the winner of Examiner's "Beer City, USA" poll for four years.

Boasting more breweries per capita than any U.S. city, the area is home to 22 craft breweries. On any given day, roughly 100 local beers can be enjoyed in Asheville, served on draft and in bottles. The scene has reached a fever pitch with five annual beer festivals, brewery tours and unusual products made from beer like shampoo, ice cream and mustard.

With 15 active farmers' markets, 250+ independent restaurants, 22 fantastic craft breweries, America's most visited winery and hundreds of inspired local products—all within a city of 85,000—Asheville is a thriving community of culinary creatives. The city gleefully blurs the lines between art, food and mountain culture, pushing the boundaries of the plate and charting new territory in craft beer, cocktails and wine. Local sake or hard cider anyone? Grab a chair and join us…

South Slope Post-Conference Excursion

Sunday, July 18 | 1-5 PM

Once populated with car lots and auto shops, the South Slope area is experiencing a renaissance. Seven breweries are already in place with another set to open this summer. Shops like Vortex Doughnuts (artisan sweets), Ben's Tune-Up (restaurant and sake brewery) and Lexington Glassworks (glassblowing gallery and studio) help diversify the area and make it Asheville's newest "must experience" area.

Organized by the Asheville Brewers Alliance, this post-conference experience will take participants on an in-depth walking tour of South Slope. Asheville Brews Cruise and Asheville Brewery Tours will provide transportation from the Four Points Asheville hotel to South Slope and back. Wear comfortable shoes. The excursion ends at 5 pm.

Registration is reserved for conference attendees and is $15. The cost includes beers, lunch and transportation. Click HERE to register.
