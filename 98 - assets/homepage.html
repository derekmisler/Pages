Welcome to Asheville, NC's official tourism web site.

Make plans to visit Asheville and find yourself immersed in our unmistakable mountain vibe. We've become well known for our culinary cool, our burgeoning beer scene and our thriving artistic community. Add to that miles of panoramic vistas and you have a recipe for an unforgettable escape from the daily grind.

Need help planning your summer escape? Explore the tales and trails of the Blue Ridge Parkway, or discover our lively downtown scene through the flavors of incredible restaurants, the rhythm of street performances and the colors of boutique shops and art galleries. Take the whole family on an adventure as you seek out local wildlife or go hunting for waterfalls. Check out our official guide to summer in Asheville.
