Welcome to the Asheville, NC's official tourism web site.

Visit Asheville during the 2012 holiday season and experience the Blue Ridge traditions that make Christmas in the mountains so special. Sit and warm your toes by the 10-foot wide fireplaces of The Grove Park Inn and explore the amazing confectionary creations of the National Gingerbread House Competition. Visit Biltmore, America's largest home, for a candlelight tour and behold a mammoth 35-foot Christmas filled with over 1,000 decorations. Or take in a festive show at one of Asheville's many holiday theater performances. A getaway to Asheville is a sure-fire way to find your jolly this holiday season.

Where to Shop

Asheville is a destination where holiday shopping is a pleasure and not a hectic race for parking spots at the mall. Looking for the perfect Christmas gift ideas for him or her? Visit Asheville's many diverse shopping districts where you can find everything from handmade Appalachian crafts to trendy clothing boutiques.
