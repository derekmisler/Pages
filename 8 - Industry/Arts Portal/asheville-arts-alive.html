Comprehensive online access to everything arts, culture, and entertainment in Buncombe County, North Carolina!

Rich possibilities abound for both art lovers and art providers in the Asheville area, including vibrant and unique festivals, cultural heritage craft and music of all kinds, fine art galleries and cutting edge theatrical performance, immersive arts experiences such as classes and hands-on fun, museums, studio tours, murals, and more. Asheville boasts a broad spectrum of creative experiences.

The Asheville Area Arts Council and the Asheville Convention & Visitors Bureau have teamed up to bring you AshevilleArtsAlive.org, a robust online resource to help showcase our area’s creative offerings and reach a broad audience of residents and visitors.

The Asheville Arts Alive website offers two main features: a calendar of events and a directory of arts amenities, artists, arts organizations and creative businesses.

Each participant will have the opportunity to create a FREE individual profile page enabling arts partners to attract visitors with images, video, events, special offers, tours, workshops, hours of operation, directions, and the opportunity to share what’s special about their unique offerings. Visitor-oriented listings may also appear on ExploreAsheville.com, the area’s official tourism website that attracts more than 3.5 million visitors annually.

Use this valuable tool to reach over 9 million visitors annually who visit Buncombe County and are looking for what is unique and special, so that they can take home great memories and more!

Criteria

  1. Is the company owner a Buncombe County Resident? Are the company's employees primarily Buncombe County residents?
  2. Is the product unique, handcrafted, and produced primarily in Buncombe County?
  3. Does the company produce ticketed cultural events?
  4. Is the company or product accessible during posted business hours?
  5. Does the company offer classes in which the public can participate?
  6. Does the company provide a regular opportunity for the public to purchase or view Buncombe County creative product?
  7. Does the company facilitate the enjoyment of Buncombe County arts, culture, and/or entertainment?
