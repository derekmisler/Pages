Asheville Restaurant Week

Battle of the Bartenders

Asheville Restaurant Week participating restaurants have been invited to participate in a cocktail competition.

The challenge:

Create a signature drink using locally produced Troy & Sons whiskey.

The reward:

Bragging rights for the best cocktail in town.

During Asheville Restaurant Week, diners are invited to try signature drinks around town and vote for their favorite at AshevilleRestaurantWeek.com.

Take a look at the videos from each mixologist making their signature drink. Videos include recipes so you can try your hand at mixing these signature drinks.
