The Grove Park Great Gatsby Giveaway

Take a trip back in time to an era complete with speakeasies, flappers and that one golden girl that got away. That's the story of F. Scott Fitzgerald's classic novel The Great Gatsby. A former guest at the famed Grove Park Inn resort & Spa, Fitzgerald spent much of his time in Asheville where his beloved Zelda was being treated at a nearby hospital.

In honor of the celebrated American novelist, the Grove Park is hosting a weekend of events surrounding Fitzgerald's birthday. Enjoy guided tours of Fitzgerald's room, fortune tellers, a cooking demonstration, chair massages, guided history tours and live vintage music throughout the resort. The event is slated for September 23-25, 2011.
