who we are, what we do, how we can help

Asheville, North Carolina is located in the Western part of the state and is situated in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains. We are a prime Southeast destination with convenient access to Interstates 40 and 26. Our Asheville Regional Airport has a growing number of flights with service to 9 major US cities. The area has 7000+ hotel rooms and meeting options ranging from 2,500-83,000 square feet. We would love to help you bring your next meeting or event to Asheville.

Who we are: Destination Gurus of the Asheville Convention & Visitors Bureau, the official Tourism Bureau for Asheville.

What we do: The Meeting and Conventions department works with all groups including associations, corporate companies, hobby enthusiasts, sporting events, family reunions and weddings. We help coordinate the details for meetings, conferences or any other events in Asheville.

How we can help:

  • Contact area hotels and meeting venues in Asheville on your behalf to gather information on rates and availability.
  • Coordinate site visits so you can better make a decision.
  • Compile area information for presentations to decision makers.
  • Assist in event planning.
  • Offer pre-convention attendance building & meeting promotion.
  • Provide visitor information & giveaways for attendees.
  • Help in creative brainstorming to make meetings in Asheville stand out above the rest.
