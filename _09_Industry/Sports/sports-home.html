Why Work with the CVB

The Asheville Convention & Visitors Bureau provides a comprehensive view of Asheville tailored to your event’s needs. Let us help you find the facilities and resources you’ll need.

Our partner, the Asheville Buncombe Regional Sports Commission, can also provide support during planning helping to ensure your sporting event is successful and memorable for all.

CVB services include:

  • Contacting hotels and venues to gather rates and availability
  • Creating itineraries for site inspections of sports venues and hotels
  • Connecting you with local service providers to plan your event

Working with the CVB in your event planning makes you eligible for additional bonus services including:

  • Asheville Travel Guides for athletes and families
  • Custom web page for your event listing hotel rates and visitor information
  • VIP amenity gifts
  • Attraction Value Cards for event attendees
  • Promotion of your public events on events calendar
  • Staffed hospitality table at event site
