Hiking the mountains of Western North Carolina

A high-elevation hike through northern hardwoods forest, the Big Butt Trail is a less traveled path and at times difficult to follow. The trail climbs to Point Misery Peak where one can see a clear sight of Mt. Mitchell and its tower on top.

  • Length: 6 total miles
  • Elevation Gain: Moderate
  • Trail Type: Out-and-back
  • Skill Level: Strenuous
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Trailhead Elevation: 5,300 feet

Directions from Asheville

  1. Take College St to NC 694N/Town Mountain Rd.
  2. After 6.3 miles, turn left onto the Blue Ridge Parkway.
  3. About 17.3 miles later, the trailhead is on the left at Cane River Gap and is marked by a forest service gate.
