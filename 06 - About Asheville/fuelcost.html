How much does it really cost?

AAA’s Fuel Cost Calculator allows you to enter your starting location and Asheville to see how many miles you’ll be traveling, how many gallons of gas you’ll use and what that costs in gas money. You just might be surprised.

For example, a trip from Atlanta to Asheville is 207 miles one way and
uses 7.14 gallons of gas if your vehicle averages 30 miles per gallon.
That means, in gas money, it only costs $24.85 to get to Asheville
from Atlanta. Here’s how much it costs from other cities:*
