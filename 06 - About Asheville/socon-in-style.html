One lucky alumni member will win accommodations for three nights, VIP tickets to each game and all the great Asheville perks listed below.

Outside of the tournament you will have the chance to explore all the pleasant surprises Asheville has to offer. Experience the unique mountain vibe, taste your way through the vibrant farm-to-table food scene and discover delicious locally crafted brew. You’ll also discover why Good Morning America named Asheville as one of the most beautiful places in America. Nestled in the oldest mountains in the world, you’ll find Asheville is your new favorite place to be.

Entry is simple, just fill out the form below and you will be entered to win. One lucky winner will be chosen at random on February 15th.
