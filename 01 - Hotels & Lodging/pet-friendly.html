Your beloved dogs and cats are welcome in pet-friendly Asheville

Pet friendly Asheville has much to offer travelers with cats and dogs.

It is quite common to see a dog lapping from a water dish next to his humans sipping coffee at an outdoor terrace. There are plenty of places for your pets to stay, ranging from pet-friendly cabin rentals and inns to hotels that allow pets. Your visiting pet can even get a massage or spa treatment! There are also several pet resorts or places to leave your pet for the day to play.
