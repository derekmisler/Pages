We Can Make Your Planning Easier

The Asheville Convention & Visitors Bureau offers a free and time saving service to help in your wedding planning. We can send the details of your wedding guests' accommodation needs to area accommodations your behalf saving you from making numerous individual phone calls. The accommodation representatives will then contact you directly with rates and details. When you are ready to secure group rates for your guests, please contact weddings@exploreasheville.com to get the process started.
