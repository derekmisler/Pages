Explore the Blue Ridge Parkway

What is there to do on the Blue Ridge Parkway? The scenic byway known as America’s Favorite Drive is the perfect summer escape, whether you want to hike among the highest peaks in the East, find stunning wildflowers and wildlife, or just enjoy the ride through one of the most beautiful National Parks. Book a room in Asheville, which offers convenient Parkway access, as well as world-class dining and shopping, local craft breweries, and a vibrant downtown to make your vacation unforgettable.
