Winter in Asheville

Frommer’s recently cited Asheville as one of the “Best Places to Go in 2015” and winter is the perfect excuse to get cozy in our Blue Ridge Mountains where the air is crisp and the sunsets are exhilarating. You’ll find our long-range mountain views—unimpeded by foliage—are even more spectacular.

Just a short drive away takes you to a variety of ski and snow tubing areas. When you’re done on the slopes, enjoy Asheville’s bustling downtown where colorful Art Deco buildings are home to restaurants, live entertainment, art galleries and a variety of fashionable shops.

Looking for a romantic getaway? Whether it’s an intimate dinner for two at one of the more than 250 independently owned restaurants, being pampered at a world-class spa, or snuggled up by an outdoor fire pit sipping a craft brew, Asheville is the place to relax, rejuvenate, and nourish your soul.
